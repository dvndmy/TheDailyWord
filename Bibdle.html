How to play

Read the verse, then guess the Bible book it comes from. Each guess reveals whether your choice matches the correct testament, section, first letter, and the book itself.

  • You can type part of a book name and choose from the suggestion list.
  • Green means exact match, gold means you are near the right book in canonical order, and red means that clue is wrong.
  • The daily mode changes by date, while the random button gives a fresh puzzle for practice.
